Participation motivation

 

We participate in clubs, and teachers encourage everyone to participate in the 2024  International  School Cyberfair.. This year's theme is to show Taiwan's life and vitality. We are in the first grade. Everyone is participating in an online blog competition for the first time. Everything is rusty. After waiting for the task to be completed, we find that we have learned a lot and broadened our horizons. Our school is located near the Zuoying District of Kaohsiung, so after school, we occasionally meet some foreign and foreign tourists. This sparked our interest in doing something for our hometown. Why don't we set up a website to introduce this historic place and let more people come here? Classmates work together, work together, and are determined to do it well.

 

Although the process of making web pages in this period was delayed from the beginning of the school until February 25 after experiencing pneumonia in Wuhan, there was a discussion at the end. The teacher had to discuss the progress of web page production with classmates using mobile LINE video. Make a lot of work for web pages.
